## Hi there, we're GAIAM 👋

We are **The Generative AI Association of Malaysia** — a community-driven organization dedicated to advancing the understanding, adoption, and responsible use of Generative AI across Malaysia.

### About Us

theGAIAM brings together developers, researchers, students, educators, businesses, and curious minds who share a common interest in shaping the future of Generative AI in Malaysia and the wider Southeast Asian region. We believe that AI is not just a technology — it's a tool that can empower individuals, transform industries, and uplift communities when guided by knowledge, ethics, and collaboration.

### What We Do

- 🤝 **Build community** — connect Malaysian AI enthusiasts, practitioners, and learners through events, meetups, and online discussions.
- 📚 **Share knowledge** — publish tutorials, guides, workshops, and open-source resources to help everyone get started with Generative AI.
- 🌱 **Support learning** — provide accessible learning paths for beginners and advanced contributors alike.
- 🛠️ **Promote responsible AI** — champion ethical, safe, and inclusive practices in how Generative AI is built and used locally.
- 🌏 **Bridge industry and academia** — foster partnerships that accelerate AI innovation in Malaysia.

### Our Focus Areas

- Large Language Models (LLMs) and applications
- Open-source AI tooling and frameworks
- AI literacy and education
- Local-language and culturally relevant AI
- AI ethics, policy, and governance
